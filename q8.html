🥑 10 Low-Calorie Snacks That Actually Fill You Up!

By Jane Doe · January 4, 2025

Hey, snack lovers! 🥳 Trying to stay on track with your health goals but can't resist munching? Don’t worry! Here’s a list of 10 low-calorie snacks that are not only delicious but will also keep you feeling full for hours. 🍴✨ Let’s dive in!

1. Greek Yogurt with Berries 🍓

Low-fat Greek yogurt paired with fresh berries is a protein-packed snack rich in antioxidants. Perfect for your cravings!

    🥄 Pro Tip: Choose unsweetened yogurt and add a drizzle of honey for natural sweetness.
  • 🌟 Amazon Pick: FAGE Total 0% Greek Yogurt – Protein-rich and creamy!

2. Hummus & Veggie Sticks 🥕

Crunchy veggie sticks like carrots, cucumbers, or bell peppers paired with hummus are a fiber-filled combo that satisfies!

    🥄 Pro Tip: Make it fancy by sprinkling paprika on the hummus.
  • 🌟 Amazon Pick: Sabra Classic Hummus – Creamy and flavorful!

3. Air-Popped Popcorn 🍿

Three cups of air-popped popcorn have less than 100 calories! It’s crunchy, satisfying, and a perfect alternative to chips.

    🥄 Pro Tip: Add a sprinkle of nutritional yeast for a cheesy flavor without the calories.
  • 🌟 Amazon Pick: Presto PopLite Hot Air Popper – Quick and oil-free!

4. Hard-Boiled Eggs 🥚

A quick and portable snack! One egg is about 70 calories and loaded with protein to keep you full.

    🥄 Pro Tip: Add a dash of black pepper or hot sauce for extra flavor.
  • 🌟 Amazon Pick: Egglettes Egg Cooker – Make hassle-free boiled eggs!

5. Apple Slices with Almond Butter 🍏

Crunchy apple slices topped with almond butter provide the perfect mix of fiber and healthy fats.

    🥄 Pro Tip: Stick to 1 tablespoon of almond butter to keep it low-calorie.
  • 🌟 Amazon Pick: Barney Butter Bare Smooth Almond Butter – No added sugar or salt!

6. Rice Cakes with Avocado 🥑

Top plain rice cakes with mashed avocado and a sprinkle of chili flakes for a savory snack that’s light but satisfying.

    🥄 Pro Tip: Add a squeeze of lemon juice for a zesty twist.
  • 🌟 Amazon Pick: Lundberg Organic Thin Stackers Rice Cakes – Gluten-free and crunchy!

7. Cottage Cheese with Pineapple 🍍

Cottage cheese is packed with protein, and pineapple adds natural sweetness and a tropical vibe!

    🥄 Pro Tip: Opt for low-fat cottage cheese to keep it calorie-friendly.
  • 🌟 Amazon Pick: Daisy Low-Fat Cottage Cheese – Creamy and fresh!

8. Edamame 🌱

Steamed edamame (young soybeans) are full of plant-based protein and are oh-so-satisfying!

    🥄 Pro Tip: Sprinkle sea salt and squeeze lime for added zing.
  • 🌟 Amazon Pick: Seapoint Farms Dry Roasted Edamame – Ready-to-eat and packed with protein!

9. Dark Chocolate & Almonds 🍫

Indulge in 1-2 small squares of dark chocolate (70% or higher) paired with a handful of almonds for a guilt-free treat.

    🥄 Pro Tip: Portion control is key—10 almonds are around 70 calories.
  • 🌟 Amazon Pick: Lindt Excellence 70% Cocoa Dark Chocolate – Rich and luxurious!

10. Chia Pudding 🌟

Mix chia seeds with almond milk, let it sit overnight, and enjoy a pudding-like texture that’s rich in omega-3s and fiber.

    🥄 Pro Tip: Add vanilla extract and top with sliced bananas for a dessert-like snack.
  • 🌟 Amazon Pick: Viva Naturals Organic Chia Seeds – Perfect for puddings!
