🍿 10 Guilt-Free Snacks Under 100 Calories!

By Jane Doe · January 4, 2025

Looking for some delicious and satisfying snacks that won't ruin your diet? 😋 We've got you covered! These snacks are perfect for when you're craving something tasty without the guilt. They’re under 100 calories, so you can munch away without worrying about overindulging. 💯 Here are 10 incredible guilt-free snacks you can enjoy anytime:

1. Air-Popped Popcorn 🍿

Popcorn is a classic snack, and when air-popped, it's super low in calories! A cup of air-popped popcorn has only around 30 calories. Toss in some herbs or a little seasoning for extra flavor!

    🛒 Amazon Pick: Presto PopLite Hot Air Popper – Great for making healthy, air-popped popcorn at home.

2. Cucumber Slices with Hummus 🥒

Crisp cucumber slices dipped in creamy hummus make for a refreshing, low-calorie snack. One cucumber has around 16 calories, and 2 tbsp of hummus adds about 60 calories.

    🛒 Amazon Pick: Sabra Classic Hummus – A popular, smooth and creamy hummus for all your snacking needs.

3. Greek Yogurt with Berries 🍓

A small serving of plain Greek yogurt with a handful of mixed berries is packed with protein and antioxidants, while staying under 100 calories!

    🛒 Amazon Pick: FAGE Total 0% Greek Yogurt – High in protein and perfect for a guilt-free snack.

4. Rice Cakes with Almond Butter 🍪

Enjoy a rice cake topped with a thin spread of almond butter for a crispy, crunchy snack. One rice cake has about 35 calories, and a small spoon of almond butter will keep you within your limit.

    🛒 Amazon Pick: Quaker Rice Cakes, Lightly Salted – Light and satisfying.

5. Carrot Sticks with Guacamole 🥕

Crunchy carrot sticks paired with creamy guacamole provide the perfect balance of taste and health. A small serving of guac (2 tbsp) and carrots is only about 90 calories.

    🛒 Amazon Pick: Wholly Guacamole Classi – The perfect, ready-to-eat guacamole!
